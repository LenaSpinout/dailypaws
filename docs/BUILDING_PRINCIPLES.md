# Building Principles

These principles guide every implementation decision in DailyPaws.

---

## 1. Protect the relationship

Every feature should strengthen the relationship between people and dogs.

---

## 2. Build the smallest thing that works

Prefer simple implementations over perfect ones.

We can always improve later.

---

## 3. Real users before perfect architecture

A real family using the app is more important than perfect code.

---

## 4. Every feature earns its place

Every feature must answer:

"How does this help a family complete their next meaningful Mission?"

If it doesn't, it probably belongs in the Backlog.

---

## 5. AI learns continuously

DailyPaws should never pretend certainty.

The AI learns through Conversations, Missions and Observations.

---

## 6. Keep the Product Model stable

Do not modify the Product Model unless implementation reveals a real problem.

Avoid changing concepts based on hypothetical discussions.

---

## 7. Prefer conversations over forms

Whenever possible, collect information naturally through Conversation.

---

## 8. Celebrate progress

DailyPaws should celebrate effort and connection rather than perfection.

---

## 9. Ship early

Working software beats perfect software.

---

## 10. Enjoy building

DailyPaws should be as enjoyable to build as it is to use.
