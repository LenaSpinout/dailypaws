# Session 010 – Mission

## Goal

Define the Mission concept.

---

## Decisions

Missions are personalised instances generated from reusable Mission Templates.

Every Mission belongs to one of four categories:

- Observe
- Practice
- Explore
- Celebrate

Every Mission contributes to at least one of:

- Learning
- Relationship
- Progress
- Meaningful Moments

Missions are generated using:

- Goal
- Dog
- Relationship
- Context
