# Session 015 – Onboarding

Participants: Founder, AI Technical Advisor

## Decision

Onboarding v1 follows a seven-step conversational flow,
documented as PD-051 in ProductDecisions.md.

Key points:

- Onboarding is the first relationship, not a registration.
- Seven steps: Welcome → dog's name → life stage →
  who am I talking to → other family members → dream cards
  (mapped to Goal Types) → Create first Mission.
- Auto-save after every answer. Resume with "Welcome back",
  never "Start over".
- Progress as "Step X of 5". Back and Skip on nearly everything.
- Deliberately excluded from v1: breed, nickname, birthday
  date picker, family roles, health data, routines.
  These are learned later through Conversations.

## Rationale

Every question must earn its place by influencing the first
Mission. Leaving gaps is intentional and aligns with
"AI should learn continuously rather than pretend to know
everything."

## Parking Lot

- Alternative opening question "Who are we getting to know
  today?" — revisit after MVP feedback.
- Family member roles (Primary caregiver etc.) — deferred.
