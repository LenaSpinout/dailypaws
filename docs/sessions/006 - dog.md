# Session 006 – Dog

## Goal

Define the Dog concept.

---

## Decisions

The Dog concept describes DailyPaws' understanding of the dog rather than objective truth.

The understanding consists of:

- Personality
- Motivations
- Capabilities
- Challenges
- Preferences

Capabilities and Challenges are always evaluated in Context.

Personality is inferred from observations rather than stored directly.

Every understanding has an internal confidence level that helps DailyPaws decide when to ask questions, generate insights and personalise missions.
