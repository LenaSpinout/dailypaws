# DailyPaws

## Small sessions. Strong relationships.

DailyPaws helps families build calm, confident dogs through everyday moments.

The goal isn't perfect obedience.

The goal is a stronger relationship between dogs and the people they live with.

DailyPaws believes that the strongest relationships are built in the small moments that happen every day.

Waiting at the front door.

Passing another dog.

Preparing dinner.

Walking to the mailbox.

Lying quietly at a café.

Those moments matter more than perfect training sessions.

DailyPaws turns those moments into meaningful missions.

---

# Mission

Help families become better teammates for their dogs.

Not through long training sessions.

But through short, enjoyable, everyday interactions.

---

# Vision

Every family should feel confident raising a calm, happy and cooperative dog.

Five minutes at a time.

---

# Product Goal

DailyPaws should feel like a supportive coach living in your pocket.

Never demanding.

Never judging.

Always encouraging.

---

# Our Philosophy

We don't train dogs.

We build relationships.

We don't chase perfection.

We celebrate progress.

Every interaction matters.

The human learns.

The dog learns.

The relationship grows.

---

# What DailyPaws is NOT

DailyPaws is not:

- a clicker training app
- a trick library
- a competition
- a habit tracker
- an obedience app
- a streak app

DailyPaws is a relationship coach for the whole family.
