# Release v0.1 – First Meaningful Experience

## Vision

Deliver the first complete DailyPaws experience.

A new family should be able to:

- Discover DailyPaws.
- Complete onboarding.
- Receive a personalised Mission.
- Complete the Mission.
- Reflect.
- Receive an Insight.
- Return the next day.

---

## Release Goals

- Validate the Product Model.
- Validate the Daily Mission loop.
- Validate the onboarding experience.
- Validate the tone of voice.

---

## Included Epics

- Foundation
- Onboarding
- Home
- Missions
- Reflection
- Insights
- Conversation (scripted or rule-based follow-up questions only —
  see PD-034/036; distinct from AI Conversation, below)

---

## Excluded

- Multiple dogs
- Multiple families
- Social
- Gamification
- Notifications
- Journey (full chaptered narrative — a simplified Timeline of
  recent activity on Home remains in scope, per MVP_PLAN.md's
  Timeline section)
- Moments
- AI Conversation (dynamic, LLM-generated dialogue — distinct from
  the scripted Conversation epic above)
