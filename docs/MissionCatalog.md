# Approved Missions

## M-001

**Status**
Draft

**Goal Type**
Everyday Life

**Mission Type**
Observe

**Mission DNA**
Discover how your dog reacts when someone arrives.

**Context**
Home

**Duration**
2–5 minutes

**Energy**
Low

**Mission**

Today's Experiment

When it feels natural, ask someone in the family to ring the doorbell or step outside and come back in.

Watch your dog's very first reaction.

There is no right or wrong.

Simply notice what happens.

**Why**

Many families believe they already know how their dog reacts.

This Mission often reveals small details they have never noticed before.
