# I-004 – Explore

Base style: see `design/prompts/base-style.md` — this prompt only
adds the scene-specific detail on top of that base.

Reference: `docs/IllustrationCatalogue.md` — I-004 Explore.

## Used On

Mission screen (Explore Missions).

## Purpose

Support Explore Missions.

## Emotion to Communicate

- Discovery
- Adventure
- Curiosity

## Scene

The default character and the default dog experiencing somewhere new
together — a path, a corner of a park, a new environment — mid-way
through discovering it, not arriving or posing for it. Per
`docs/MissionFramework.md`'s Mission Types, Explore means "experience
a new place, situation or perspective," so the composition should
favour movement and openness (more outdoor whitespace, a sense of
somewhere to go) over a static, settled scene.

Per `docs/IllustrationGuide.md`'s Mission Illustrations principle,
this should represent the *feeling* of exploring together, not a
literal map, route or destination.

## Avoid

- Maps, arrows, routes or any navigational/technical device.
- Anything that reads as travel logistics rather than shared
  discovery.
