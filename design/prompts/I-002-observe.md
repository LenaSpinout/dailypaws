# I-002 – Observe

Base style: see `design/prompts/base-style.md` — this prompt only
adds the scene-specific detail on top of that base.

Reference: `docs/IllustrationCatalogue.md` — I-002 Observe.

## Used On

Mission screen (Observe Missions).

## Purpose

Support Observe Missions.

## Emotion to Communicate

- Curiosity
- Awareness
- Patience

## Scene

The default character quietly watching the default dog do something
that happens naturally — sniffing, looking around, noticing its
surroundings — without directing, calling or interacting. Per
`docs/MissionFramework.md`'s Mission Types, Observe means "notice
something that happens naturally," so the person's body language
should read as attentive and patient, not passive or bored.

Per `docs/IllustrationGuide.md`'s Mission Illustrations principle,
this should represent the *feeling* of observing, not a literal
depiction of any single Observe Mission's exact instructions.

## Avoid

- The person actively doing something to or with the dog — this is
  about watching, not participating.
- Any hint of surveillance, testing or evaluation.
