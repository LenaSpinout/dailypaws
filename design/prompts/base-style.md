# DailyPaws Base Illustration Prompt

Create a warm, Scandinavian-inspired editorial illustration.

Style:

- hand-drawn vector illustration
- clean white background
- thin outlines
- muted earthy colours
- warm beige, sage green, soft brown and terracotta accents
- minimal details
- soft rounded shapes
- calm composition
- lots of whitespace
- timeless design
- premium rather than childish

Mood:

- calm
- curious
- optimistic
- emotionally warm
- peaceful
- friendly

Characters:

- ordinary adults
- relaxed body language
- medium-sized brown doodle-like dog
- natural dog behaviour
- subtle facial expressions

The illustration should communicate emotion rather than explain the activity.

Avoid:

- cartoon style
- exaggerated expressions
- gamification
- bright saturated colours
- busy backgrounds
- technical diagrams
- training equipment
