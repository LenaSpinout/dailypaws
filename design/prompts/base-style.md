# DailyPaws Base Illustration Prompt

Create a clean, friendly flat illustration in the approved DailyPaws
"Illustrated & Friendly" style.

## Core visual style

- Clearly hand-drawn cartoon illustration
- Simple, readable shapes
- Distinct dark outlines
- Mostly flat colour fills
- Very limited texture
- No realistic rendering
- No watercolor look
- No detailed editorial-book realism
- Warm and expressive without feeling childish
- Scandinavian simplicity with generous whitespace

## Mood

The illustration should feel:

- Warm
- Friendly
- Inviting
- Calm
- Positive
- Playful but mature
- Easy to understand at a glance

## Characters

Use ordinary, relatable adults with casual clothing and relaxed body language.

For the Explorer Release, use:

- one adult woman as the default person
- one medium-sized brown doodle-like dog

The dog should have clear personality and readable body language.

The dog may look:

- curious
- pleased
- thoughtful
- slightly surprised
- calmly excited

The dog should be more expressive than realistic, but never exaggerated or chaotic.

## Line and shape

- Use clean dark outlines around people, dogs and key objects
- Use rounded, simplified forms
- Keep facial features simple and expressive
- Avoid fine anatomical detail
- Avoid soft painterly edges
- Avoid complex shading
- Avoid realistic fur rendering

## Colour

Use a warm, muted palette:

- sage green
- warm ochre
- terracotta
- soft blue
- warm brown
- cream
- off-white

Colours should be clear and flat, with only very subtle shading where necessary.

## Backgrounds

- Clean white or warm off-white background
- Minimal environmental details
- One or two simple supporting elements such as a tree, flower, cloud, bench or grass patch
- Plenty of whitespace
- No detailed scenery

## Emotional focus

Illustrations should communicate the relationship between the person and dog.

They should feel like a friendly modern picture book for adults and families.

Illustrate emotion and interaction rather than technical instruction.

## Avoid

- realistic or semi-realistic painting
- watercolor texture
- detailed fur
- soft editorial realism
- cinematic lighting
- gradients
- complex shadows
- crowded scenes
- exaggerated Disney-style expressions
- childish proportions
- training equipment as the main focus
- text or UI elements inside the illustration
