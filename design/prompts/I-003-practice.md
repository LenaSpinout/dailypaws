# I-003 – Practice

Base style: see `design/prompts/base-style.md` — this prompt only
adds the scene-specific detail on top of that base.

Reference: `docs/IllustrationCatalogue.md` — I-003 Practice.

## Used On

Mission screen (Practice Missions).

## Purpose

Support Practice Missions.

## Emotion to Communicate

- Learning together
- Encouragement
- Confidence

## Scene

The default character and the default dog trying something together
— calm, shared effort, side by side. Per
`docs/MissionFramework.md`'s Mission Types, Practice means "try
something together without focusing on success or failure," so
nothing in the scene should read as a test, a trick being performed,
or a pass/fail moment.

Per `docs/IllustrationGuide.md`'s Mission Illustrations principle,
this should represent the *feeling* of practicing together, not a
literal depiction of any single Practice Mission's exact
instructions.

## Avoid

- Training equipment, commands, or any obedience-exercise framing.
- Visual cues that imply correctness, scoring or performance.
