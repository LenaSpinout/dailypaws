## Scene

Show the Explorer Release default adult woman kneeling or crouching on the grass, facing the default medium-sized brown doodle-like dog.

The dog sits and looks at her with friendly, visible curiosity.
The woman smiles warmly back.
Their eye contact should feel natural and mutual.

Include only:
- a small simple tree,
- one tiny flower,
- one simple cloud,
- a subtle small heart between them.

Use generous whitespace.
No text or UI elements.

## Intended feeling

"We're about to discover something together."

The relationship should feel warm, friendly and playful, not formal or training-oriented.

## Scene-specific exclusions

- no leash
- no training equipment
- no commands or performance pose
- no crowded environment
- no extra people or animals
