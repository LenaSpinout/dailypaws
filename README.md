# DailyPaws

> **Small sessions. Strong relationships.**

DailyPaws is an AI companion that helps families build extraordinary relationships with their dogs through tiny everyday moments.

Rather than focusing on obedience alone, DailyPaws helps families better understand their dogs, strengthen their relationships and enjoy everyday life together.

Every conversation, mission and insight is designed to make life together a little better.

---

# Vision

Helping families build extraordinary relationships with their dogs.

---

# Philosophy

DailyPaws is built around a few simple ideas.

- Relationships before obedience.
- Small sessions create lasting change.
- Curiosity beats perfection.
- Every dog is unique.
- Every family is unique.
- AI should learn continuously rather than pretend to know everything.
- The best training happens naturally in everyday life.

---

# Product Principles

DailyPaws is a relationship app, not a dog training app.

Every feature should help users do one or more of the following:

- Learn something about their dog.
- Strengthen a relationship.
- Move towards a meaningful goal.
- Create a memory worth keeping.

If a feature does none of these, it probably doesn't belong in DailyPaws.

# Working Principles

Development follows a few simple rules.

- Documentation before implementation.
- One session = one decision.
- Build only from approved documentation.
- New ideas go to the Parking Lot.
- Protect the relationship.

---

# Sprint 1 Goal

Build the smallest possible version of DailyPaws that allows a family to:

- complete onboarding,
- receive a personalised Mission,
- reflect on the experience,
- receive their first Insight.

Success is defined as:

> A family can complete their first meaningful DailyPaws Mission within approximately ten minutes of installing the app.
